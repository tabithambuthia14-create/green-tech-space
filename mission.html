Our Mission

Green Tech Space exists to bridge the gap between technology, climate justice, and human rights. Our mission is to empower young people, policymakers, and communities across the Global South to understand, shape, and govern emerging technologies in ways that protect people and the planet.

We focus on responsible innovation, ethical AI, data governance, and digital tools that advance sustainability, accountability, and environmental justice.
